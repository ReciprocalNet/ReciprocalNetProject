Fungicide

Chemicals you may find:

Captan Acetic acid
Fungicides are chemicals that prevent the growth and spread of unwanted fungi that tend to damage and/or kill crop and garden plants. However they may be potential health hazards to pets and humans. There are two types of fungicides, contact and systemic. Contact fungicides kill when sprayed on the fungi while systemic fungicides kill when absorbed into the fungi. Fungicides that are used in homesteads and gardens are for example captan and acetic acid, among others.
