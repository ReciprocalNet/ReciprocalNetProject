Site Accessibility

The Reciprocal Net Site pledges to develop and maintain a Web environment that is accessible to everyone. We are committed to complying with the recommendations from the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) and the standards created by the Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (Access Board) as part of Section 508, a 1998 amendment to the United States Rehabilitation Act of 1973.

While our efforts to remove barriers to access are continuous, we welcome your comments and suggestions on how to improve our Web presence.
