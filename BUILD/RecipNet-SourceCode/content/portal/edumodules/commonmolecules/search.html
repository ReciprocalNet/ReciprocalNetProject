Find Common Molecules



  You can find specific common molecules. There are over 500 molecules available in our collection.
If you don't find the molecule you are looking for, you may try alphabetical list of common molecules in our collection and more advanced molecular structure search on Reciprocal Net. Or you may send us your request.




  Tip : Enter a compound name or a part of it.
The compound name may be a name by which the substance is know or a trade name or the IUPAC name (the name according to the International Union's of Pure and Applied Chemistry nomenclature).
