Rubbing Alcohol

Chemicals you may find:

Isopropanol
Water
Methanol

Rubbing alcohol typically contains isopropyl alcohol and water and sometimes a small amount of methyl alcohol. Rubbing alcohol is commonly used to sterilize surfaces or, when mixed with water alone, to clean minor cuts or abrasions. This liquid evaporates quickly and is therefore cooling and soothing the skin. However, rubbing alcohol is poisonous when ingested and can cause permanent disabling illness or death.
