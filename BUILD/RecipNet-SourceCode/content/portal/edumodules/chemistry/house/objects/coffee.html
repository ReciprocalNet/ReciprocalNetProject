Coffee is a drink that is prepared by extracting flavors from roasted, ground coffee beans (harvested from the coffee plant) using hot water. Historically, the beverage came from Africa to the Middle East and now is consumed throughout the world. There are many ways to make coffee and the taste of the beverage depends on the brewing procedure, including the temperature and the time of extraction, but also on the mineral content of the water, the darkness of the roast, and the grind size. Coffee is a stimulant for the nervous system and increases the blood pressure.
