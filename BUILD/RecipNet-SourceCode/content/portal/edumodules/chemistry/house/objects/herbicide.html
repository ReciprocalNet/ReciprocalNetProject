Herbicide

Chemicals you may find:

2,4-D
Trimethyamine
Mecoprop
Diuron
Juglone

Herbicides are pesticides that are used to kill weeds and other unwanted plants, however they may also be harmful to other life including pets and humans. These chemicals work by inhibiting the growth of the plant and are widely used in agriculture. There are various herbicides in use today that include 2, 4-D, also known as Amidox, mecoprop and others.

2, 4-D is used to kill broadleaf plants that grow amongst grasses but this herbicide has little selectivity and often kills non-target plants. Glyphosphate is another herbicide that is also used to kill weeds and is now more commonly used against plants that have developed a resistance to the herbicides developed in earlier decades.

Juglone is one of the naturally occurring herbicides. It is in all plant parts of the Black Walnut and stunts the growth of nearby plants by inhibiting their metabolism.
