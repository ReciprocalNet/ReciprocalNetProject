Nitroglycerin

Correct! Nitroglycerin is a powerful explosive and an important ingredient in most forms of dynamite. Alfred Nobel developed a safe and profitable manufacturing process and sold a combination of nitroglycerin and gunpowder as "Swedish Blasting Oil." In very small amounts, nitroglycerin is used as a medicine in treating patients with certain heart conditions; it acts as a blood vessel relaxant and thus lowers the blood pressure.
