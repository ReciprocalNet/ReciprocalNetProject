Partner Sites

One component of Reciprocal Net is a distributed database for crystallographic information.  The Reciprocal Net Site Network links participating service crystallography laboratories across the nation to form a massive collection of structure data.  Molecular structures at all participating sites can be found using Reciprocalnet.org's distributed search engine.
